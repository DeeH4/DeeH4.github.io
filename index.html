ABOUT ME

I was born and raised in Christchurch and lived most of my life there, I experienced two of the biggest earthquakes in the history of New Zealand in 2010 and 2011, I continued to live there until 2016, when I had to move to Wellington to study design at Massey University.

I am currently majoring in Spatial Design, and these photographs here are from a mixture of Christchurch, for my design and photography folio's in highschool and Wellington, for the Lens paper that I took during summer school this year.
